Examples: "0 3 * * *" (daily 3am), "0 */6 * * *" (every 6 hours), "0 3 * * 0" (weekly Sunday 3am), "0 3 1 * *" (monthly 1st 3am)
When enabled, this job will run according to its schedule. Disable to temporarily pause scheduling.
When enabled, this job will wait for other jobs using the same source or destination to finish before running.
